# Competencies Evaluation App

I want to create a web application that can evaluate skills and competencies of employees and candidates.
The skills and competencies are divided into categories.

* Professional skills
* Soft skills
* Readiness to learn and change
* AI adoption readiness  
* Technical skills
* Management skills
* Leadership skills
* Communication skills
* Teamwork skills
* Problem-solving skills
* Time management skills

For each category, there are several competencies. Each competency has a set of questions that evaluate the level of proficiency of the employee or candidate.

For now we will start evaluating:
* Professional skills
* Soft skills
* Readiness to learn and change

We will have different professional profiles, each with its own set of competencies and questions.

We will start with:
* Técnico en Seguros de Salud y Vida.
* Técnico en Seguros de Riesgos Generales, Responsabilidad Civil y Fianzas.

The app should be multilingual, let's start with Spanish and English.

# Our Competencies Model is as follows:

## Step 1: Evaluation
The candidate completes a survey with questions, interactive exercises, simulations and case studies, related to the competencies of the professional profile.
This should be able to be evaluated by the app or AI.

## Step 2: Analysis 
The app/AI reviews the candidates' responses to the questions and:

### Calculate 3 ObjectiveIndices
Objective evaluation of the employee or candidate (from 0 to 100):
* Professional skills (knowledge, skills, and abilities on the topic)
* Soft skills
* Readiness to learn and change

### Identify Strengths and Opportunities for Growth
* List specific areas of strength
* List specific areas for growth
Note: We call weaknesses or debilidades "Oportunidades de Crecimiento" to keep a positive approach.

## Step 3: Assign Profiles
Based on the objective indices, strengths and opportunities for growth, assign the candidate to the most appropriate profiles.
* Assign the candidate to the most appropriate professional profile.
* Assign the candidate to the most appropriate soft skills profile.
* Assign the candidate to the most appropriate readiness to learn and change profile.

These are profiles we have created in advance to facilitate the diagnostics and recommendations.
Users may exhibit traits of different profiles and combine them. The app/AI should be able to indicate this.

### Step 4: Recommendations
The app/AI should generate recommendations based on the analysis, including the following sections:
* Mensaje personalizado unificador que resume los resultados y las recomendaciones
* Ruta de capacitación personalizada
* Recomendaciones de actividades y recursos
* Estrategias de gestión del cambio
* Apoyos para fortalezas y debilidades
* Recomendaciones para perfiles combinados

We will provide a list of courses, activities, resources, and strategies to help the candidate improve their skills.
The recommendations should be personalized and tailored to the candidate's strengths and opportunities for growth.
The recommendations should refer to the courses, activities, resources, and strategies provided.
